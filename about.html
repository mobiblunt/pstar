    We are passionate about the Oil and Gas business

    Pstar services Ltd is an offshore drilling contractor that operates and manages a fleet of modern, high specification drilling rigs on a worldwide basis. Pstar services Ltd provides advanced drilling solutions to the energy industry. We are a technically innovative company providing worldwide drilling services, rental tools and project management, including rig design, construction and operations management.

    Since our inception we have forged a very experienced and dynamic team of personnel from across the globe to support our worldwide Drilling Operations. The success of Pstar services Ltd will largely be attributed to its transformation from a provider of technical solutions to that of a trusted advisor to clients.

    Our Speciality

    We leverage our technical and safety leadership, cultivated through decades of project execution in new and emerging global markets, to enhance the efficiencies of our customers' drilling programs.
